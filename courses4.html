CPR/AED Certification

For the community and workplace

When a life is on the line, every second counts. Our CPR Professional Rescuer Certification course is designed for those who need advanced CPR skills, such as healthcare providers, first responders, and other professionals. This comprehensive training teaches you how to perform high-quality CPR, use an AED, and manage airway emergencies for adults, children, and infants.

$24.95

  • CPR Professional (Adult + Child + Infant)
  • AED (Automated External Defibulator)
  • Choking Techniques + Recovery Position
  • 2 Rescuer CPR + Breathing Bag Training

Overview

In this course, you will acquire essential life-saving skills, including advanced chest compressions, effective ventilation techniques, and the proper use of an automated external defibrillator (AED). Through expert instruction and practical, hands-on training, you will gain the proficiency and confidence needed to respond swiftly and effectively in emergency situations. Tailored for first responders, this course covers specialized techniques and advanced methods beyond the scope of our community and workplace courses. Drawing on over 100 years of collective safety training experience, the program incorporates the latest industry standards and protocols, as established by certified professionals in the field.

Whether you are obtaining certification for the first time or renewing, this course delivers the most up-to-date best practices in a clear and easily retained format. This course is designed to be completed at your own pace, allowing you to pause and resume as needed, with an estimated completion time of one to two hours. Certifications are available for immediate download upon successful completion of the exam and remain valid for two years from the date of issuance.

  • An introduction to CPR for the professional responders
  • Overview of Good Samaritan Laws and their relevance to healthcare providers
  • Understanding the chain of survival and your critical role in providing care
  • Adherence to universal precautions to ensure your safety
  • CPR techniques for adults, children, and infants
  • Two-rescuer CPR methods for inviduals of all ages
  • Chest-encircling CPR technique for infants
  • Compression-only CPR
  • Integration of a bag-valve mask (BVM) durring CPR
  • Proper use of an Automated External Defibrillator (AED)
  • Correct positioning of a victim in the recovery position
  • Protocols for managing conscious and unconscious choking victims
  • Specialized techniques tailored to advanced care providers
