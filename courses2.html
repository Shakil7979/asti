Overview

Knowing how to perform cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) and use an automated external defibrillator (AED) is crucial and can often be lifesaving. In our CPR Certification class for the Community and Workplace course, you will learn how to properly and safely perform CPR on adults, children, and infants, as well as how to use an AED for individuals of all ages. This course is designed to provide easy-to-follow, step-by-step instructions that you can rely on during an emergency, whether at work or in everyday life.

This course is suitable for both first-time certification candidates and those seeking recertification. The course materials were developed by a team of industry experts and medical professionals, and include videos, images, and detailed descriptions of each step. Upon completing the CPR online course and passing the exam, you will have access to download digital certification. Certificates are valid for two years.

Our goal in creating this online CPR training course is to offer a low-stress learning environment, allowing students to progress at their own pace. You can pause and resume the course as needed or complete it in a single session, which typically takes no more than one hour. If you do not pass the exam on your first attempt, you may retake it at no additional cost.
