Frequently Asked Group Questions

What sets ASTI apart from other online CPR certification providers?

The American Safety Training Institute boasts one of the largest instructor networks in the United States, with over 100 years of combined experience in safety and health training. Our team consists of highly qualified medical professionals, first responders, and certified safety instructors. Furthermore, we are one of the few nationally recognized training organizations that offer flexible course delivery options, including online, on-site, and blended formats.

Is your certification accepted by my agency / employer?

American Safety Training Institute certifications are fully aligned with the latest guidelines from the International Consensus on Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (ICCR) and Emergency Cardiovascular Care Treatment (ECC). With our money-back guarantee, we ensure that every student receives top-quality training with complete peace of mind.

How long does it take to complete an online certification course?

On average, our students complete courses in 1 to 3 hours. The CPR Certification and First Aid Training courses typically take around one hour each. Our training programs are designed for flexibility, allowing you to progress at your own pace. Should you need to take a break, you can easily log out and return to the course at your convenience.
