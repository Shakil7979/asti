Online Courses

The American Safety Training Institute provides nationally recognized online CPR certification courses, delivering essential life-saving skills through easily accessible, high-quality training. We have designed user-friendly online CPR courses that equip you with the essential techniques and knowledge required to pass the certification exam, obtain your CPR certification, and immediately print your two-year certification card. Adhering to the most current ECC/ILCOR Guidelines, our courses ensure that your certification will be both up-to-date and widely recognized.
