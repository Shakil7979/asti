Overview

Our BLS course encompasses comprehensive CPR certification training for Professional Rescuers, First Aid, Bloodborne Pathogens, and Emergency Oxygen Administration. Ensure you're prepared to act confidently in critical situations with our Basic Life Support (BLS) certification program. Designed for healthcare professionals and anyone looking to enhance their emergency response skills, this course teaches essential techniques to stabilize and support patients in life-threatening scenarios.

Through clear and concise content this course provides essential skills in CPR, AED, First Aid, Bloodborne Pathogen safety, and Emergency Oxygen Administration. You will learn to perform high-quality chest compressions, deliver effective ventilation, use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED) correctly, and respond appropriately to medical emergencies, all in line with the latest industry standards. Upon successful completion, you will receive immediate access to your wallet cards and digital certification, ensuring you are fully equipped to handle life-threatening situations with confidence and skill.

Enroll today to gain the critical skills needed to provide effective life-saving support and ensure the well-being of those in your care. Certification is valid for two years.
