Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions, and we have the answers. With over 100 years of combined expertise in health and safety training, the American Safety Training Institute is committed to providing top-tier CPR certification courses. Our programs equip you with the essential skills and knowledge to respond to emergency situations with confidence.

What sets ASTI apart from other online CPR certification providers?

The American Safety Training Institute boasts one of the largest instructor networks in the United States, with over 100 years of combined experience in safety and health training. Our team consists of highly qualified medical professionals, first responders, and certified safety instructors. Furthermore, we are one of the few nationally recognized training organizations that offer flexible course delivery options, including online, on-site, and blended formats.

Is your certification accepted by my agency / employer?

American Safety Training Institute certifications are fully aligned with the latest guidelines from the International Consensus on Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (ICCR) and Emergency Cardiovascular Care Treatment (ECC). With our money-back guarantee, we ensure that every student receives top-quality training with complete peace of mind.

How long does it take to complete an online certification course?

On average, our students complete courses in 1 to 3 hours. The CPR Certification and First Aid Training courses typically take around one hour each. Our training programs are designed for flexibility, allowing you to progress at your own pace. Should you need to take a break, you can easily log out and return to the course at your convenience.

Is it required to complete a course in a single session within the student portal?

No, our training is designed to offer maximum flexibility, allowing you to learn at your own pace. Participants enjoy unlimited access to our training portal, enabling you to review course material and complete the online exam at your convenience. Should you need to take a break, you can easily log out and return to the course at any time. This approach ensures that you can complete the training in a way that fits your schedule, providing a seamless learning experience.

I have a large group to register. What is the process for registering multiple participants at once?

Our group registration process is straightforward. Simply provide us with your list of participants, and we will handle the registration for each individual. Once registered, we will send you activated usernames and initial passwords via email. For billing, we will issue a single invoice for the entire group, ensuring all participants can access the training materials simultaneously. Groups of five or more participants are eligible for a group rate discount. Please contact ASTI for more details.

What is the validity period of my certifications?

American Safety Training Institute certifications remain valid for two years from the date of issuance.

What can I do if I do not pass the ASTI training exam?

No worries! The cognitive exam can be retaken as many times as necessary, at no extra cost. Our goal is to ensure you thoroughly understand the material, not just pass the exam. All test questions are multiple choice or true/false, based directly on the course content. Participants will have unlimited access to the online training materials after registration, allowing you to review and prepare at your own pace.

Is your certification accepted by my agency / employer?

American Safety Training Institute certifications follow the most up-to-date guidelines provided by the International Consensus on Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (ICCR) and the Emergency Cardiovascular Care with Treatment (ECCT) recommendations. All students are fully protected by American Safety Training Institute's money-back guarantee.

How soon after registration can I take a course?

Once your registration and payment are successfully processed, you will gain immediate access to the online course materials. Upon completing the sign-up process, your billing account will be charged, and your login credentials will be activated, allowing you to begin the course right away.

I need my certification immediately, how fast will I get it?

Upon successful completion of the course, you will be able to immediately download your certificate and wallet card(s) directly from your account. This ensures that you have instant access to your certification, allowing you to keep a digital copy on hand and print your wallet card for convenience.

Do you offer courses in languages other than English?

Yes, we offer all of our safety training programs in Spanish. Once you've registered, simply log in to your course and switch the language preference from English to Spanish to begin your training in your preferred language.

Can international students take this training?

Yes! Our training programs adhere to international safety standards, allowing participants from anywhere in the world to complete their courses. We proudly train thousands of individuals globally. If you encounter any issues with registration from outside the U.S., please don't hesitate to contact our live chat support for assistance.

How do I renew my existing CPR Certification?

Our safety training programs are designed for both first-time participants and those seeking recertification. With our self-paced training, you can easily progress through the content at your convenience. When you're ready, simply proceed directly to the certification exam. To renew your certification, visit our sign-up page and select the training option that best suits your needs.

Are there any additional costs?

Our online training programs include the digital certificate, downloadable wallet cards, and comprehensive training covering adult, child, and infant techniques—at no additional cost. Unlike other providers who charge extra for certification cards or other services, we include everything in our price. Please note, blended training assessments may require a small additional fee to cover the instructor's time. For specific pricing on blended training courses, please contact us, as fees may vary by location.

If you have any additional questions, feel free to reach out to our live chat support or visit our Contact Us page. Our dedicated American Safety Training Institute team is ready to assist you.
