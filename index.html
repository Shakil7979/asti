Online Training

Our online certification courses offer an effective way to enhance your life-saving skills and apply proven first aid techniques. These programs feature step-by-step video demonstrations, easy-to-follow reading materials, and detailed images, designed to help you retain and apply the information effectively. We offer training in CPR, AED, first aid, emergency oxygen administration, and non-medical bloodborne pathogen treatment. Accessible 24/7 from any device with internet access, our courses can typically be completed in less time than it would take to drive to an in-person session.

Upon successful completion, you can immediately download your certificate and wallet card(s).

On-Site Training

Our on-site certification programs provide instructor-led courses that are engaging, informative, and educational. ASTI's highly experienced instructors meet all ASTI and industry standards, ensuring the highest quality training. On-site classes typically take 3-5 hours to complete and include an instructional booklet, video demonstrations (where available), and hands-on training. ASTI is proud to have one of the largest and most qualified instructor networks in North America, providing flexible training options for organizations across the region. Whether you are a small business, large corporation, or community group, our team is ready to meet your training needs.

Blended Training

Our blended certification programs seamlessly combine the flexibility of online learning with the benefits of instructor-led, on-site training. Participants begin by completing the online course materials and then attend a condensed, in-person session at your facility for hands-on skills training. The on-site portion typically lasts about one hour, ensuring that participants have ample time to practice and refine their skills under the guidance of our expert instructors. This combination of online learning and in-person instruction provides a comprehensive, efficient, and engaging learning experience that equips participants with both the theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for certification.
